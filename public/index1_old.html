FAQ

What is this?

We're scanning the internet and social media to build a complete list of things you can do, listing the closest ones first. You can re-sort and filter if you'd like.

How do I post my event

Tweet the details of your event, include #EVSTK and a date (11/23, 11/23/15, 13 Jan). We'll reply back asking for an address. If you've posted previously, we already have your address. Here's an example:

Yoga in the park tomorrow morning at 8am sharp, 4/23. Start your weekend off right at Thomas Park near 5th - $8. #EVSTK #yoga #fitness

— Paul (@eventstackco) April 22, 2016

That's it. Check eventstack.co for your metro and you'll see your event listed. See below for more examples.

How can I tweet the location of my event?

If your event tweet includes our #EVSTK and a date, we'll tweet back asking for the address of the event. In your reply, include:

  • #EVSTK - this gets our attention
  • #ADD, followed by the address of your event. This is required for us to include your event in the Stack.
  • #PHO, followed by the phone number for your event. This is optional.
  • #URL, followed by the url for your event. This is optional.
  • #IMG, followed by the full path to an image for your event. If none is provided, a default image will be selected by our algorithm and included on our site with your posting.

We'll save that address and use it for every event your tweet after that. Here are a few sample address tweet:

#EVSTK #ADD 1000 N. Sheffield Chicago, IL

— Paul (@eventstackco) April 22, 2016

#EVSTK #ADD 2000 N. Ashland Chicago, IL #URL https://t.co/ch01tH6vS5 #IMG https://t.co/mOGZXGQPuE #PHO 773-123-4567

— Paul (@eventstackco) April 22, 2016

How can I categorize my event?

We are scanning your event tweet for hashtags like: #kids #music #art #free #fitness

I need to take my event down

If you (your twitter handle) posted the event, you can remove that event from the stack by tweeting #EVSTK #DELETE #ID . Here's an example:

@eventstackco 58dc3d66fa50bdd24bc05159 Thanks for posting to EventStack. We have your address. Your event is listed!

— Paul (@eventstackco) March 29, 2017

#EVSTK #DELETE #ID 58dc3d66fa50bdd24bc05159

— Paul (@eventstackco) March 30, 2017

@eventstackco This event has been removed:58dc3d66fa50bdd24bc05159

— Paul (@eventstackco) March 30, 2017

That's it. Check eventstack.co for your metro and you'll see your event listed. See below for more examples.
