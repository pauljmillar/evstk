  • 01

    How to post your event

    Tweet the details of your event, include #thingstodo and the event date (these formats work: 11/23, 11/23/15, 23 Nov). We'll reply back instantly asking for the address of the event. If you've posted previously, we already have your address.

    That's it. Check daily.bz and you'll see your event listed in your city.

    As of Jan 2016, we're only accepting listings for the Chicago or Charlotte metro areas.

  • 02

    How to categorize your event

    Daily includes dozens of filter categories for easy sorting of events and activities. You can categorize the event you post by including any of the pre-approved hashtags:

    #kids, #music, #family, #free, #outside, #art, #fitness, #theater

    As of Jan. 2016, we're currently only applying the 'kids' filter. The rest will follow soon.

  • 03

    How to edit your post

    Simply tweet from the same twitter handle, with the same event date. The body of the new tweet will replace the old one.

  • 04

    How to request a new metro area

    Visit daily.bz/contact, suggest the new hashtag, and click submit. Or you can email cities@daily.bz.

  • 05

    Everything else

    Please send us a note via daily.bz/contact, or send an email to questions@daily.bz.
